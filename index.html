Use public issues for product feedback that can be discussed openly. Keep private account, billing, uploaded media, logs, and vulnerability details out of public tickets.

For private support or security-sensitive reports, contact the Nano Products shared official support mailbox at contact@gencoloring.ai. Production source code, provider credentials, billing configuration, and deployment secrets are not published in the public repository.
